Tracklite

13/4/2017 1:29:26 PM.

The Tracklite is designed to accommodate the needs of construction ironworker using cranes to lift the steel columns for erecting a building structure.

Thus the main functionality manifest in the ruggedness and durability of the flashlight.

Thermoplastic rubber is majorly used because these materials are lightweight but can also withstand damages from falling with high strength. The rubberized tube also indicates the tactility of the interaction and extends the service life of the flashlight, with tolerance to high temperature and risky daily use of construction workers. This is a group project from IAT 336. We followed the whole process of iteration: sketching, prototyping, modeling, finalizing. After we decided which sketch we would work on, we made a physical model at first. Since we got a lot of feedbacks from critique session for improving the functions, we began to build digital prototypes in Maya. Finally, we got the finalized project done and made the poster.
