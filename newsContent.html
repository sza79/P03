Originally created as a variation on the classic Thonet bent-wood tradition the Duet Chair is actually made from a series of carved wood components, revealing a stunning composition of grain patterns. Once carved, each component is joined with mortise and tenon for a seamless, stable frame. Designed by neri&hu and manufactured by De La Espada for the neri&hu brand.Originally created as a variation on the classic Thonet bent-wood tradition. Flemming Lassen were responsible for the entire town hall aesthetic appearance, from the design of the entire building to furniture and small details such as door handles. Mayor shows a moderation of modernism and a unique regional adaption: the frame is made of massive oak, the upholstery is tight but with three loose cushions that comprise the seating area.
